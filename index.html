About

I grew up in Mumbai, spent my school years in Rajasthan, and returned for college, choosing finance because it felt like the safe answer.

Then came a 3-month internship at Frapp in 2019, where I ran campaigns for Amazon, Flipkart, Spotify, OYO, and Starbucks.

That's where things shifted.

Watching real users take real actions because of something I built — that was the moment marketing stopped being a subject and became the thing I wanted to do.

After college, when agencies didn't respond, I co-founded Avroan Wholesales — a physical wholesale business. No funding, no safety net. We crossed ₹1.8 crore in sales within 10 months, grew 15% month-over-month, and had to pause orders because we outgrew our capacity.

That taught me something I still carry: most brands try to scale before they've earned the right to — chasing growth before anyone deeply loves what they've built.

I believe in getting the sequence right — earn love first, then scale.

Currently at Wondrlab, scaling influencer marketing for 35+ D2C brands. Also building Digital Chetan — helping small businesses solve their real growth problems.
